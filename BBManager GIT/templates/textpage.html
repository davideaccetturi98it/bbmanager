Welcome to our Bed & Breakfast SMART CHECK-IN system!


Hoping you're enjoying this technological way of checking in, we would like to tell you something more about this innovative idea.
Everything started trying to find a comfortable and flexible solution for guests who are entering their few-days house.
Hence, our aim was to be in contact with you even if we were not able to be in town at your arrive.

We studied this system by which we manage to open the house door from away, just giving you a combination of rings you should perform with the intercom.

You can enter the house to rest a bit after a long journey, without having to wait until our arrive!
You don't have to think about the keys: you will never forget or lose them because you will never need them!


It sounds great, doesn't it?
Everything is ready, now you just need to try this system... Have an amazing stay!
